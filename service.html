Our Services

Speech Therapy Services

Speech Sound Disorders

Assessment and treatment for articulation and phonological disorders affecting speech clarity.

Difficulty pronouncing certain sounds, unclear speech, frustration when not understood, and avoidance of speaking situations.

Through individualized therapy targeting specific sound errors, oral-motor exercises, and carryover activities for daily communication.

Stuttering & Fluency Disorders

Comprehensive management for stuttering, cluttering, and other fluency challenges.

Stuttering has genetic, neurological, and environmental components. It often runs in families and affects the coordination of speech muscles.

Audiology Services

Hearing Assessment

Comprehensive diagnostic testing including Pure Tone Audiometry (PTA), Otoacoustic Emissions (OAE), Brainstem Evoked Response Audiometry (BERA), and Tympanometry.

Hearing Aid Fitting

Personalized selection, fitting, and programming of hearing aids with follow-up care and adjustments.

Child Development Services

Autism & Developmental Delay

Early intervention and therapy for children with autism spectrum disorder and developmental challenges.

Language Development

Assessment and therapy for language delays and disorders in children.
